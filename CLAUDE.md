## Critical Architectural Principles

- Clean architecture (clear separation of concerns)
- Vertical slice thinking where appropriate
- DRY
- SRP
- Avoid WET
- Core logic must live in reusable application/domain layers

## Final Constraints

- Do NOT fake functionality
- Do NOT skip real implementation
- Do NOT overcomplicate agent system
- Keep it minimal but real
- Focus on correctness over completeness
